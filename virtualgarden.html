Aloe vera



Botanical Name - Aloe barbadensis miller.
Common Names - Aloe vera, Barbados aloe, First Aid Plant.
Habitat - Aloe vera is native to the Arabian Peninsula but is widely distributed and grows wild in tropical, semi-tropical, and arid climates around the world. It thrives in hot, dry climates and is cultivated in subtropical regions globally. ... Medicinal Uses - Aloe vera has a variety of medicinal uses, including:
Topical Applications: Used for treating burns, skin abrasions, insect bites, and minor cuts. It is also used in lotions, ointments, and gels for its soothing properties.


Tulsi



Botanical Name - Ocimum tenuiflorum (also known as Ocimum sanctum).
Common Names - Tulsi or Holy Basil.
Habitat - Tulsi, also known as holy basil (Ocimum sanctum), thrives in warm, tropical climates. It prefers well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight, making it ideal for gardens or pots in sunny locations. ... Tulsi is commonly found in India and other Southeast Asian countries, often grown in homes for its religious and medicinal significance. It flourishes in temperatures between 20°C to 30°C (68°F to 86°F) and requires moderate watering.
